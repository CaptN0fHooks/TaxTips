## Gap Description

The CTR documents the federal termination of §25C and §25D credits after 12/31/2025 (OBBBA §70504/§70505). The CTR does not explicitly address California's conformity or non-conformity to this termination for the 2025 California return. California's January 1, 2025 conformity date (SB 711) means CA does not pick up OBBBA generally, but it is unclear whether CA independently maintained §25C/§25D equivalents that survive the OBBBA termination.

## Interim Action

`AMB-2025-CA-ENERGY-CREDITS` created in ca-540-2025 with a conservative default: do not claim CA energy credits on the 2025 return until FTB issues guidance. Tax Director sign-off on this disposition required.

## Amendment Requested

Add an explicit CTR note in the next Controlling Report revision addressing CA §25C/§25D conformity for tax year 2025. Alternatively, Tax Director provides written confirmation (committed to this file) that the conservative default is the correct operative posture.
