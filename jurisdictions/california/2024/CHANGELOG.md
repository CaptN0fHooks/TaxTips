### Key 2024 Changes
- Standard deduction: Single/MFS $5,540; MFJ/HoH/QSS $11,080
- CalEITC: max $3,644; earned income cap $31,950; investment cap $4,674
- YCTC: max $1,154; phase-out starts $26,626
- SDI: wage cap ELIMINATED per SB 951; rate 1.1% on ALL wages/tips
- UEBE mileage: 67 cents per mile
- No section 224 addback (pre-OBBBA year)
