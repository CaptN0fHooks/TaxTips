# D7 Scenario Coverage - california/2024

Canonical scenario format is JSON.

Minimum gate: 50 complete scenarios, 3 commercial differential scenarios, zero sentinel values, Tax Director sign-off on every scenario.

## Required Coverage

- Base coverage: filing statuses, deduction choice, tips/Form 4137, multi-W-2, excess Social Security.
- California UEBE: uniforms, non-slip shoes, tools, union dues, combined expenses with floor, and mileage between jobsites.
- California standard vs itemized: standard optimal, itemized with UEBE optimal, itemized without UEBE.
- CalEITC/YCTC: qualifying, child under 6, ITIN-eligible case.
- SDI withholding reconciliation, including no wage cap treatment where applicable.
- California nonconformity: HSA addback, disaster loss addback, mortgage-interest difference.
- No Section 224 addback scenarios for this pre-OBBBA year.
- Commercial differential: at least 3 scenarios verified against TurboTax, H&R Block, or TaxAct.

Specialists author all inputs, expected outputs, CTR anchors, commercial results, and Tax Director sign-off fields.
